My Personal Interests

I love to socialize with others through cooperation and/or competition.

I love to play skillful video games where your own improvement is based more on learning than spending time and collecting experience/items.

Specifically when it comes to video games, I ended up playing a lot of a Super Nintendo game called Super Metroid (while I was at home) since that was one of the only ways I could meet people (even if usually only online) and socialize when I worked the 10:00 P.M. to 8:00 A.M. midnight shift monitoring at the hospital. I began to speedrun (beat as fast as possible) Super Metroid and competed in several events playing and commentating in marathons with 100,000+ live viewers. Making great lifelong friends and achieving several world records was quite an experience.


For sports, table tennis is my favorite, but I love regular tennis as well. I like golf in a scramble format, basketball is fun, and I used to think I was good at being a soccer goalie until I realized just how small of a pond I came from.


I love music and movies, but otherwise I'm up for trying almost anything new that doesn't trigger my significant fear of heights.
