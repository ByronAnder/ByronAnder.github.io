Academic Accomplishments

I grew up learning in Concord from kindergarten all the way until I graduated high school.

My goal to get accepted to the University of Michigan from Concord was usually mistaken for a joke since we were just a small farm town and my backup school was inevitably what people would wonder about. Answering with complete honesty was the most fun because there was no back-up. I wanted to go to The University of Michigan and I put in the work to make it happen.
